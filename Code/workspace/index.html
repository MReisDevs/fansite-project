Matt's Fansite to Darkest Dungeon

General Information

Darkest Dungeon is a "Dungeon Crawling" style video game developed by Red Hook Studios. The game was initially funded through a successful Kickstarter campaign in April of 2014, the game was released in Early Access first to Kickstarter backers on January 30, 2015 and then through Steam Early Access February 2, 2015 and developed continued, using information gathered from gameplay videos and livestreams and feedback to help fine tune and polish the final product. The game reached full release on January 19, 2016 on Windows PC and later that year on PS4, Vita, and Linux.
In this game, you take on the persona of the inheritant of an estate in a state of disrepair and housing a dark secret. To uncover the truth of your inheritance you delve into to the caverns and tunnels beneath and surrounding your ancestral home seeking both treasure and truth and aided by other explorers, however each dungeon could be your last as dangers lurk with every step, waiting to drive your party to the brink of insanity and ultimately, death's door. Survive and you might regain your family's wealth and restore the hamlet surrounding your family's home. Fail and the humiliation of death shall be your only reward.

Introduction Monologue

Ruin has come to our family.
You remember our venerable house, opulent and imperial, gazing proudly from its stoic perch above the moor.
I lived all my years in that ancient rumor shadowed manor, fattened by decadence and luxury, and yet I began to tire of... conventional extravagance.
Singular unsettling tales suggested the mansion itself was a gateway to some fabulous and unnameable power.
With relic and ritual, I bent every effort towards the excavation and recovery of those long buried secrets, exhausting what remained of our family fortune on... swarthy workmen and... sturdy shovels.
At last, in the salt soaked crags beneath the lowest foundations, we unearthed that damnable portal of antediluvian evil.
Our every step unsettled the ancient earth, but we were in a realm of death and madness.
In the end, I alone fled, laughing and wailing through those blackened arcades of antiquity.
Until consciousness failed me.
You remember our venerable house, opulent and imperial, it is a festering abomination.
I beg you, return home, claim your birthright and deliver our family from the ravenous clutching shadows of the Darkest Dungeon.
